hlock
=====

Once upon a time  
Hardlock with [16F84]  
Encrypting data

Cycles counted fast  
To communicate abroad  
Powered by the port

[Op-amp] brought the power in  
But the connection was weak  
Frequent deaths occurred

In a plastic mold it sat  
Fragile and vulnerable  
A story comes to end
